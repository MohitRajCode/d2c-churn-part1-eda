# Business Memo: Customer Retention Strategy

## Key Findings

1. Customers with lower web activity are more likely to churn.
2. Higher support ticket counts are associated with increased churn.
3. Customers with lower spending show higher churn probability.
4. Loyalty programs appear to improve customer retention.
5. High return rates indicate greater churn risk.

## High-Risk Customer Groups

* Low engagement customers
* Customers with many support interactions
* Non-loyalty members
* Customers with high return rates

## Recommendations

1. Launch re-engagement campaigns for inactive users.
2. Improve customer support efficiency.
3. Increase loyalty program enrollment.
4. Analyze product return reasons.
5. Offer personalized retention incentives.

## Retention Priorities

Priority should be given to customers exhibiting:

* Low sessions
* High ticket counts
* No loyalty membership
* High return behavior
